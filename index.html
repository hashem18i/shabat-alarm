Privacy Policy for Shabbat Alarm

Last Updated: February 11, 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Shabbat Alarm ("we", "our", or "app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information Collection

Shabbat Alarm is designed with your privacy in mind and collects minimal personal information:

  • We do not collect any personal identification information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect any user data
  • All alarm settings are stored locally on your device

App Permissions

Our app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Alarm Permission: To set and trigger alarms at your specified times
  • Storage Permission: To store alarm sounds locally on your device
  • Vibration Permission: To provide vibration alerts with alarms
  • Run at Startup Permission: To restore your alarms after device restart

Data Storage

All data, including alarm settings and preferences, is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store any of your information on external servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app does not use any third-party services or analytics tools that would collect user data.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app is suitable for all ages as it only provides alarm clock functionality.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
